# Pickup Pelunasan

- Command: `/wh-ticket`
- Kategori: `Pindah Fisik`
- Ticket value: `pickup_pelunasan`
- Channel env: `CHANNEL_PINDAHFISIK`
- Thread prefix: `PICKUP`

## Field

- Wajib: `store_name`, `order_id`, `assigned_to`
- Opsional: `note`

## Cara Buat

1. Jalankan `/wh-ticket`
2. Pilih `Pindah Fisik`
3. Pilih `Pickup Pelunasan`
4. Pilih `Store Name`
5. Isi `Order ID`
6. Pilih minimal 1 assignee lalu submit

## Catatan

- Opsi store: `alpha`, `beta`, `gamma`
