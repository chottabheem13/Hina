# Adjust Stock (QTY)

- Command: `/warehouse-ticket`
- Kategori: `WH Stock Management`
- Ticket value: `adjust_stock_qty`
- Channel env: `CHANNEL_WHSTOCKMANAGEMENT`
- Thread prefix: `ADJ-QTY`

## Field

- Wajib: `item_id`, `notes`, `assigned_to`
- Opsional: `order_id`

## Cara Buat

1. Jalankan `/warehouse-ticket`
2. Pilih `WH Stock Management`
3. Pilih `Adjust Stock (QTY)`
4. Isi `Item ID`
5. Isi `Notes`
6. Pilih minimal 1 assignee lalu submit

## Catatan

- `order_id` opsional jika perubahan stok terkait order tertentu
