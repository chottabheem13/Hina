# WH Pick Other

- Command: `/warehouse-ticket`
- Kategori: `WH Pick`
- Ticket value: `wh_pick_other`
- Channel env: `CHANNEL_WHPICK`
- Thread prefix: `WH-PICK`

## Field

- Wajib: `item_id`, `notes`, `assigned_to`
- Opsional: `order_id`

## Cara Buat

1. Jalankan `/warehouse-ticket`
2. Pilih `WH Pick`
3. Pilih `Other`
4. Isi `Item ID`
5. Isi `Notes`
6. Pilih minimal 1 assignee lalu submit

## Catatan

- `order_id` bisa diisi bila request terkait order tertentu
