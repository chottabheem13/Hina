# Campaign

- Command: `/mulmed-ticket`
- Kategori: `Promotional Design`
- Ticket value: `campaign`
- Channel env: `CHANNEL_PROMO`
- Thread prefix: `CAMPAIGN`

## Field

- Wajib: `brief`, `assigned_to`
- Opsional: `priority`, `link`

## Cara Buat

1. Jalankan `/mulmed-ticket`
2. Pilih `Promotional Design`
3. Pilih `Campaign`
4. Isi `Brief`
5. Pilih minimal 1 assignee
6. Submit modal

## Catatan

- Brief sebaiknya memuat deadline, size, dan info tambahan
