# Wallpaper

- Command: `/mulmed-ticket`
- Kategori: `Single Printing`
- Ticket value: `wallpaper`
- Channel env: `CHANNEL_SINGLEPRINT`
- Thread prefix: `WALLPAPER`

## Field

- Wajib: `brief`, `size`, `assigned_to`
- Opsional: `priority`, `link`

## Cara Buat

1. Jalankan `/mulmed-ticket`
2. Pilih `Single Printing`
3. Pilih `Wallpaper`
4. Isi `Brief`
5. Isi `Size`
6. Pilih minimal 1 assignee lalu submit

## Catatan

- `size` wajib di modal ini
